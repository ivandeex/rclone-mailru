### Advanced options

Here are the advanced options specific to icloud (Apple iCloud Drive).

- Config:      user_agent
- Env Var:     RCLONE_ICLOUD_USER_AGENT
- Type:        string
- Default:     ""

This sets the encoding for the backend.
